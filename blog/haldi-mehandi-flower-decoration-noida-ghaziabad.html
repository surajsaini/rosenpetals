The Haldi Mehandi ceremony is a pre-wedding ritual that is typically observed in Hindu, Sikh, and Muslim weddings in India. It is usually held a day or two before the wedding day and is a fun-filled event where the bride and groom, along with their families and friends, come together to apply haldi (turmeric paste) and mehndi (henna designs) on their bodies.

Flower decoration plays a crucial role in any wedding ceremony, and the Haldi Mehandi ceremony is no exception. It is a joyful and vibrant event where families and friends of the bride and groom come together to celebrate the upcoming wedding. The right flower decoration can add to the beauty and charm of the occasion and make it even more memorable.
