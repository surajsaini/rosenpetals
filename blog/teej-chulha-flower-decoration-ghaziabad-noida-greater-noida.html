About Teej Festival

Teej festival is dedicated to Goddess Parvati and Lord Shiva. Married women and young girls celebrate it by observing fasts, praying for the long life of their husbands, and enjoying swings decorated with flowers. It usually falls in the monsoon season (July–August). A decorated chulha is an important part of the rituals, which is why flower decoration plays a big role in this celebration.
